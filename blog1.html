Introduction

The SSC CGL 2026 examination is one of the most competitive exams in India, with over 30 lakh applicants every year. A well‑structured strategy can make all the difference between selection and rejection. Here's a month‑wise plan to ace both Tier‑I and Tier‑II with confidence.

🔑 Key Fact: SSC CGL Tier‑I 2026 is expected in June‑July. Start now to get 3 months of solid preparation.

Month 1 – Foundation (May – June)

This is the most critical phase. Without a strong foundation, advanced practice won't help. Focus on understanding concepts, not just memorizing formulas.

Quantitative Aptitude

  • Week 1‑2: Percentages, Profit & Loss, Averages, Ratio & Proportion
  • Week 3: Simplification, Number System, HCF/LCM
  • Week 4: Speed, Time & Distance, Time & Work

English Language

  • Practice 10 comprehension passages daily from previous year papers.
  • Master grammar rules: Articles, Prepositions, Subject‑Verb Agreement.
  • Learn 20 new vocabulary words every day with synonyms & antonyms.

Reasoning

  • Focus on: Analogies, Odd‑One‑Out, Series, Coding‑Decoding.
  • Practice puzzle solving for 30 minutes daily.

General Awareness

  • Read daily current affairs from TestMasterPro's notes.
  • Revise static GK: History (Modern India), Geography, Polity, Science.

Don't try to cover everything in Month 1. Depth over breadth – master 70% of the syllabus deeply rather than skimming 100%.

Month 2 – Intensive Practice (July)

Now that your basics are clear, it's time to apply your knowledge under exam‑like conditions. This is where most aspirants see the biggest score jump.

  • Attempt free mock tests on TestMasterPro.space every alternate day.
  • Analyze your weak areas using the detailed result dashboard – don't just check scores, study the solutions.
  • Start full‑length SSC CGL Tier‑I mocks under timed conditions (60 minutes exactly).
  • Revise static GK: history, geography, polity, and science using quick notes.
  • Practice Data Interpretation (Bar graphs, Pie charts, Tables) – 5 sets daily.

📊 Target: By end of Month 2, you should consistently score 130+ in Tier‑I mocks.

Month 3 – Final Touch (August – Till Exam)

The last phase is about speed, accuracy, and mental stamina. Your knowledge is solid – now make it lightning fast.

  • Take 2 mocks per day (morning & evening) and review every mistake.
  • Focus on high‑weightage chapters: Trigonometry, Algebra, and Data Interpretation.
  • Use the Daily Challenge feature on TestMasterPro to improve speed.
  • Practice previous year questions (at least last 5 years).
  • Create a formula sheet for quick revision – go through it every morning.
  • Stay consistent and avoid learning new topics in the last week.

In the final 7 days: focus ONLY on revision + 1 mock/day. Don't exhaust yourself. Sleep 7‑8 hours. Your brain consolidates memory during sleep!

Pro Tips & Resources

  • 📱 Download TestMasterPro mobile app for practice on‑the‑go.
  • 📝 Maintain an error notebook – write down every mistake you make in mocks.
  • ⏰ Study in 45‑minute focused blocks with 10‑minute breaks (Pomodoro technique).
  • 👥 Join Telegram groups for daily quizzes and peer motivation.
  • 🏋️ Exercise 30 minutes daily – improves focus and reduces exam anxiety.
